I graduated from Universidad Católica "Nuestra Señora de la Asunción" in Paraguay with a MSc in Computer Science, specialising in many-objective optimisation using the Particle Swarm meta-heuristic.

During my undergraduate years I worked in web development at Arsis and CBA until 2015, when I joined the PaccanaroLab group as a PhD Student under the supervision of Prof. Alberto Paccanaro.

I maintain collaborations with parasitologists and experimental biologists in Paraguay, studying tropical diseases such as Chagas disease, a parasitosis caused by the Trypanosoma cruzi.
